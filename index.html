Who is My EV Report for?

Primarily franchised and independent dealers, car supermarkets and fleet operators who want a clear, independent view of battery health when buying or selling EVs.

How long does the process take?

Once the test has been carried out, we aim to deliver the final report within 72 hours. In many cases it will be sooner, depending on volumes.

Can I use the report in my adverts?

Yes. Reports are designed to be used in your online listings, handover packs and aftersales conversations – they’re written in plain language for customers.

Do you offer white-label or bulk terms?

We can provide white-label branding and volume pricing for dealer groups, buying services and fleets. Mention this when you contact us and we’ll talk through options.
