Why battery health really matters

The battery is the most expensive part of an electric vehicle. Two cars with the same age and mileage can have very different battery health – which means very different value for money.

  • • A weaker battery can mean less range than you expect.
  • • Poor health can affect future resale value.
  • • Warranties and guarantees often depend on measured battery health.
  • • A clear, independent report gives you leverage when negotiating price.
In short:

My EV Report helps you answer the question every EV buyer should ask:

“Is this battery as good as the advert suggests?”

Instead of guessing from mileage or relying on a dashboard estimate, you get real data, clearly explained.
